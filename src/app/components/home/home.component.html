Pico y Placa rules

Pico y Placa is a system that allows to reduce the traffic in Quito. This method was taken from the similar system that has been working in Bogota. Be aware that if you get caught driving your car in the time and date that you are not allow, you will be fined with a penalty (see table).

You can also use the parking spots that are located at the streets that are the limits of the "Pico y Placa". To see the name of the streets ,please go to map to find more details.
